What StreamCut Pro Does

🎤

Offline Transcription

Whisper-based transcription runs locally on your machine. No cloud API fees, full privacy. Supports multiple languages and model sizes.

⚡

AI Viral Moment Detection

Gemini AI analyzes your transcription to find the most engaging, shareable moments: clutches, fails, emotional peaks, and meme-worthy clips.

🎬

Automated Rendering

GPU-accelerated rendering via FFmpeg with animated subtitles, title overlays, custom templates, and vertical 9:16 formatting for Shorts/Reels/TikTok.

📷

AI Thumbnail Generation

Generates eye-catching thumbnails using Gemini image models with smart fallback chain. Uses real video frames, mascots, and reference images.

🚀

Auto Pipeline Mode

One toggle enables full automation: upload → transcribe → analyze → render. Smart checkpoints pause for your decisions on clip selection and metadata.

📊

Multi-Platform Publishing

Publish long videos to YouTube and short clips to YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikTok. OAuth integration with token auto-refresh.

Publish Everywhere

Connect your accounts and publish clips directly from the app to all major platforms.

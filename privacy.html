StreamCut Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how StreamCut Pro ("the Service") collects, uses, stores, and discloses information when you use the app or its related pages.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Service, the following categories of data may be processed:

  • Video, audio, subtitle, title, project, and editing data you choose to process.
  • Connected-platform authorization data, such as YouTube, Instagram, or TikTok tokens.
  • Basic technical data, such as timestamps, logs, device or browser metadata, and error diagnostics.
  • Public profile data returned by third-party platforms when you authorize access.

2. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Provide the requested video-editing, subtitle-generation, publishing, and account-linking features.
  • Authenticate connected social accounts and maintain active sessions.
  • Improve reliability, debug technical issues, and protect the Service from misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce platform or developer requirements.

3. Local Processing and Temporary Storage

StreamCut Pro is designed for local or developer-controlled use. In many deployments, media files and generated assets are processed locally on the device or environment where the app is running. Temporary files, render outputs, and stored social tokens may be kept in local or server-side temporary storage for functionality until deleted, disconnected, or overwritten.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service may rely on third-party APIs and platforms, including AI providers and social networks such as Google/YouTube, Meta/Instagram, and TikTok. When you choose to connect those services, your information may be processed according to their privacy policies and developer platform terms.

5. Legal Bases and Consent

Where applicable, information is processed based on your consent, the need to perform the service you requested, legitimate interests in operating and securing the Service, or compliance with legal obligations.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only as needed to:

  • Operate requested third-party integrations.
  • Respond to lawful requests or protect legal rights and security.
  • Support infrastructure, hosting, or processing necessary to deliver the Service.

7. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including active sessions, temporary rendering workflows, troubleshooting, and legal compliance. You can also disconnect connected accounts or remove local data from your deployment environment where applicable.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection regarding your personal information. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and it is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards may be used to protect information, but no storage or transmission method is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your own environment, devices, and third-party account credentials.

11. International Data Transfers

If you use the Service across jurisdictions, information may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to applicable law and the practices of the third-party services you choose to connect.
